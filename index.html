About Our Camp

[The Camp With No Name] is lead by experienced burners and is always a mix of veterans, virgins and orphans alike. We welcome burners from all over the world to create an interactive art camp with a strong international presence and one of the best dive bars in the suburbs.

Amenities Include:

  • Large shade structure
  • Public lounge and private camp sitting area
  • Fully-equipped kitchen
  • Shower stall with evaporation pond
  • Mackie Thump sound-system
